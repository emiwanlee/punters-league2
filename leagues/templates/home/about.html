About Punters League

Punters League is a dynamic football insights platform built for fans, punters, and analysts who crave accurate and engaging league predictions. We analyze recent form, home/away performance, and other real-world data to offer you reliable forecasts for matches across Europe, Asia, and Australia.

Whether you're a passionate supporter or a seasoned tipster, Punters League helps you stay ahead of the game with:

  • Up-to-date league tables and fixtures
  • BTTS (Both Teams To Score) and Over/Under tips
  • Team performance visualizations
  • Match previews and football blog posts

Our goal is to create a community-driven space where analysis meets passion. With tools built using Django and JavaScript, and a design focused on simplicity and speed, we’re committed to providing value without the clutter.

This project started as a learning journey and evolved into a resource for fans who want more than just match scores. As we grow, we’ll continue to listen, improve, and add features that serve you better.

Thank you for being a part of the Punters League journey.
